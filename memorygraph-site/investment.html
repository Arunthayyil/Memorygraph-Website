Common Questions

Things couples ask.

Why don't you list fixed prices?
+

Because no two weddings are the same. A 200-guest temple wedding in Thrissur and a 40-guest beach ceremony in Varkala have completely different needs. We'd rather quote you what your wedding actually requires than force you into a generic tier.

How far in advance should we book?
+

Because we only take 12 weddings a year, dates fill up quickly — particularly between October and February. We recommend reaching out 6-9 months in advance, though we occasionally have last-minute openings.

Do you travel for destination weddings?
+

Yes — gladly. We've covered weddings across South India and would love to do more. Travel and accommodation are handled separately and quoted transparently.

What's the deposit and payment structure?
+

A 30% retainer secures your date. The remaining balance is split — 50% one week before the wedding, 20% on delivery. Everything in writing, everything transparent.

When do we get our photos and films?
+

Sneak peeks within 48 hours. Full edited gallery in 10-14 days. Cinematic film in 30-45 days depending on length and complexity. Albums take an additional 4-6 weeks.

Are you DGCA-certified for drone coverage?
+

Yes. All our aerial coverage is conducted by DGCA-certified pilots with appropriate permissions for the location. Safety and legality are non-negotiable.
