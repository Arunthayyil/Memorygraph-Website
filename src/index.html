The Studio

We don't measure
the weddings we've shot.
We measure the ones we don't forget.

"Every couple deserves a team that isn't running on autopilot — that shows up rested, listens carefully, and leaves with photographs you'll keep returning to."

Memorygraph is a small Thrissur-based studio led by photographer-filmmaker Arun T Chandran, with a tight team of collaborators who believe wedding photography has become too much about production and not enough about presence. Documentary in spirit. Cinematic in craft. Quietly devoted to every wedding we hold in our hands.

Documentary Cinematic Editorial Kerala & Beyond

Three crafts, one story.

Photography

We shoot the way memory works — quiet, unforced, drawn to the moments people don't realize they're making. Editorial in look. Documentary in soul.

Cinematography

Wedding films that feel like short cinema — your real voices, your real silences. Built to be watched again and again, not scrolled past.

Aerial & Drone

Sweeping aerial frames of your venue, the procession, the landscape — the kind of scale your wedding deserves to be remembered at.

The Experience

How we work.

From the first message to the final album — here's what it feels like to work with Memorygraph.

01
The First Conversation

A 30-minute call — not a sales pitch. We listen to your story, your vision, your worries. Most couples leave already feeling lighter.

02
A Plan Built Around You

Light maps, ceremony flow, family logistics, drone permissions. We over-plan so the day itself can be effortless.

03
The Day Itself

Quiet, observant, deeply focused. We move like shadows — present everywhere, intrusive nowhere. You'll forget we're there.

04
Delivered with Care

Curated galleries, cinematic films, heirloom-quality albums. Delivered within 30 days, presented beautifully — yours forever.
