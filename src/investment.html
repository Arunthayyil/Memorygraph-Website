How It Works

From first hello
to final frame.

No complicated steps, no mystery timelines. Here is exactly how a Memorygraph wedding unfolds — from the moment you reach out to the day your gallery arrives.

1
A quiet conversation

You write to us. We reply within 24 hours. No sales pitch — just a genuine conversation about your day, your family, and how you want it remembered.

2
Booking & planning

Choose your collection, sign a simple agreement, and pay a 50% advance to hold the date. We plan shot lists, timelines, and logistics together.

3
Your wedding day

Arun leads every wedding personally. We arrive early, stay late, and move quietly — capturing moments as they unfold, never staging or interrupting.

4
Editing & delivery

Within 30 days, your private gallery goes live — hand-edited photographs, film links, album selection tools, all in one place. Highlight teasers arrive within 48 hours.
