Common Questions

Things couples ask.

Will Arun himself shoot my wedding?
+

Yes. Arun leads every Memorygraph wedding personally — from the first call through to delivery. He doesn't hand off shoots to a second team. The person you meet before the wedding is the person behind the camera on the day. Additional team members (second shooter, cinematographer) are introduced to you in advance so there are no surprises.

What does a typical Memorygraph wedding cost?
+

Most couples book The Story collection at ₹1,00,000 — it's our most complete offering and covers everything: two days, a pre-wedding shoot, full documentary film, and a printed album. Coverage starts at ₹75,000 for one-day bookings. The Legacy collection at ₹1,50,000 adds the Heirloom Album and drone coverage for weddings that want every angle. Multi-day and destination weddings are quoted after an initial conversation.

Can we customise what's included in a collection?
+

Yes. The collections are starting points — if your wedding has a different shape (three-day ritual cycle, multiple venues, outstation travel), we'll build a proposal around what it actually needs. Write to us with the details and we'll respond within 48 hours.

How far in advance should we book?
+

Wedding dates fill quickly — particularly between October and February, which is Kerala's peak wedding season. We recommend reaching out 6–9 months in advance. That said, we occasionally have late openings, so it's always worth asking even if your wedding is soon.

Do you travel for destination weddings?
+

Yes — gladly. We've covered weddings across South India and would love to do more. Travel and accommodation are handled separately and quoted transparently — no hidden costs.

What's the deposit and payment structure?
+

A 30% retainer secures your date. The remaining balance is split — 50% one week before the wedding, 20% on delivery. Everything in writing, everything transparent.

When do we get our photos and films?
+

Sneak peeks within 48 hours. Full edited gallery in 10–14 days. Cinematic film in 30–45 days depending on length and complexity. Albums take an additional 4–6 weeks to produce and print.

Are you DGCA-certified for drone coverage?
+

Yes. All aerial coverage is conducted by DGCA-certified pilots with appropriate permissions for your specific location and venue. Safety and legality are non-negotiable — we handle all the paperwork.

When exactly will we receive our photos and film?
+

We work to a four-week delivery promise. Sneak peeks arrive within 48 hours of your wedding. Your full edited gallery follows within two weeks. The cinematic wedding film is delivered within four weeks — not four months. Albums take an additional 4–6 weeks to produce and print. We hold this timeline as seriously as the wedding day itself.

What happens if a camera fails on our wedding day?
+

It won't affect your wedding — we come prepared for this. Every camera shoots to two SD cards simultaneously, so every frame is written to two separate cards the moment it's taken. We bring full backup camera bodies on standby. All footage is transferred and backed up to multiple drives before we leave your venue. In eight years of shooting weddings, redundancy has never been optional for us — it's standard.
